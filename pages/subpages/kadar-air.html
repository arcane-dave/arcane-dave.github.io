ABSTRACT
Analysis of water content in food is important in the food dry or fresh food. The aim of this lab is to train the practitioner to analyze the water content of some samples of food (green beans, soybeans, peanuts, corn, and rice) with a drying method (thermogravimetric) is good and right, from sample preparation, drying, to weighing and calculation of the water content of the samples were given. This laboratory method is thermogravimetric with a drying oven. The results of each sample obtained water content of dry, wet moisture content, total solids and water content in foodstuffs in open cup and saucer covered. The practical conclusion is praktikan analyze the water content of some samples of food (green beans, soybeans, peanuts, corn)
Keywords: Analysis of Moisture, thermogravimetric, Oven.
